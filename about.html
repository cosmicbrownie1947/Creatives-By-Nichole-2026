About Me

I am a Digital Marketing student with a strong focus on content creation, branding, and analytics-driven strategy. My experience includes photography, videography, and marketing across both corporate and freelance environments.

Community Statement

As a digital marketing student and media specialist, I am an active member of the evolving world of the creative industry. My experience goes beyond the brands and businesses I collaborate with, and into the broader digital audience that consumes media day to day. My work in photography, videography, and marketing has allowed me to move beyond the role of a student and build an exceptional professional profile with tangible skills. I find myself especially connected to brands that aren’t sure how to spread their voice, tell their story, and expand their reach. My role in that is to uplift them and get them to a new level they did not realize was possible. My participation in my projects and communities was rooted before I began college, and I believe the knowledge I’ve gained before, during, and soon to be after will help me to continue to develop a strong understanding of different audience behaviors, branding strategies, and ethical media practices. The foundation has been strengthened over time by my real-world experiences working with brands like Ruger, Hornady, and Winchester, where I have created content that aligns with their brands while also engaging their target audience. Working with these brands has allowed me to collaborate with industry professionals, understand the expectations, and recognize the responsibility that comes with influencing people and their purchase habits through my media. As a content creator, I feel myself drawn to the work of professional collaboration, but also a part of a growing community that uses its digital platform to share news, build business, and connect. I believe it is on my part to make sure that the audience isn’t being given misinformation, a lack of authenticity, and oversaturation of content by creating content that is meaningful, useful, and engaging for those watching. I feel it is my duty to address any issues and produce high-quality content that people will want to watch and engage in. I have engaged best with my community by leaning away from the bigger businesses and putting focus and attention into the smaller companies, the brands that need that voice on digital platforms but aren’t sure how to get it. I have been working with local small businesses in my area, helping them to find and develop that voice that speaks to their audience. I plan to expand on this involvement by working more closely with small businesses and local entrepreneurs who may not have access to their creative marketing resources. By offering my creative direction, understanding their KPI, and working on their social media campaigns, I believe I can help these businesses communicate their values more effectively. In addition to business-focused contributions, I am also committed to being a responsible digital citizen. This includes maintaining an ethical standard that doesn’t use Artificial Intelligence to generate all of its content and respects intellectual property. By promoting inclusive and respectful communication online, I believe I can carry this awareness into every project I take on and ensure that my work continues to benefit the community to the best it can. My undergraduate experience has also encouraged me to think more about the role of media in society and the impact I can have as a creator. One of my key takeaways from undergraduate school is to approach problems with both creativity and intention. To research, brand, and plan each project and create meaningful work that is not about the aesthetics or performance metrics, but about creative value and fostering connection. Looking ahead towards the future, I want to continue building a career that merges my creativity with purpose. I want to aim to be more a part of communities that prioritize innovation, collaboration, and positive impact. Whether that means supporting small businesses, contributing to a larger network, collaborating, or making an impact, I want to approach every step with intention and the best possible outlook. Ultimately, I believe that building a community starts with being that community and learning to outreach intentionally, ethically, and meaningfully. This is the standard I strive to uphold in both my professional and personal life.
